Kyle Moon and the Misled

Independent Texas Country Music

Kyle Moon & the Misled is an Americana/Cosmic Country outfit based in San Antonio, Texas. Kyle Moon honed his songwriting skills in the Little Ravens, an Indie/Americana band in Denver, Colorado. As a group, they garnered a huge following, and sold out venues such as the Marquis Theater in downtown Denver. At the height of their popularity, Kyle moved to San Antonio, Texas where the band “Kyle Moon & the Misled” began to take shape in mid 2018. By the beginning of 2019 they released their first EP “Goodbye Denver” with stand-out tracks, “One Shot”, “Goodbye Denver” and “Texas Summer” which received regional radio airplay. In August 2019 the band released their first full length record, “Straight Fine Livin’”. The lead-off single "Paint this Town Red" is a Cosmic, Bluesy ode to the good life, while the second single, "Dance Halls" draws its inspiration from what makes Texas Music so unique. Other standout tracks include; the old-fashioned barnburner that is the title track "Straight Fine Livin'", "Stay Young" a ballad about growing up and growing old, and the moody groove of "Worst Enemy." Though they only have been playing around San Antonio for a little more than a year the group’s live show has built them a solid reputation amongst concert goers and brought big crowds to many local venues such as Fitzgerald’s and the legendary Sam’s Burger Joint.
